Problem Statement

"Develop a CV for detecting vehicle defect manufacturing ."

This project is about building a smart camera system that can automatically find problems in cars while they’re being made. Quality control teams will use this system to check for issues like scratches, dents, or parts that aren’t lined up properly. The goal is to make sure only perfect cars leave the factory, reducing the need for manual checks and ensuring customers get high-quality vehicles.

What

Develop a computer vision model that can automatically detect manufacturing defects in vehicles, such as scratches, dents, misalignments, and other anomalies, during the production process.

Where

The quality control team in automotive manufacturing companies, responsible for ensuring that vehicles meet the highest standards before they reach consumers.

When

This solution is needed during the vehicle assembly line and final inspection stages to prevent defective vehicles from being shipped to customers, thereby reducing recalls and improving overall product quality.

Why

Implementing an automated defect detection system will enhance the efficiency and accuracy of quality control, reduce the reliance on manual inspection, minimize production delays, and ensure that only defect-free vehicles are delivered to customers.
