Problem Statement

"Develop a Statistical Model to Predict Vehicle Maintenance Needs"

This project focuses on building a model that can predict when a car will need maintenance, based on how it’s been used. The model will be useful for car owners, maintenance teams, and fleet managers to help them plan and schedule maintenance ahead of time. By predicting issues before they happen, this model will help prevent unexpected breakdowns, save money, and keep cars running longer.

What

Develop a statistical model that predicts when a vehicle will need maintenance based on usage data.

Where

Vehicle maintenance teams, fleet managers, and car owners.

When

Ongoing, with predictions made throughout the vehicle’s lifecycle to anticipate and schedule maintenance.

Why

To prevent unexpected breakdowns, optimize maintenance schedules, and extend the lifespan of vehicles.
